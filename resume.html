Follow these tips to create a standout resume!

1. Create a Compelling Career Summary

Start with a strong summary that highlights your strengths, skills, and aspirations to capture the recruiter’s attention.

2. Focus on Relevant Experience

Include any internships, volunteer work, or part-time jobs that align with your career goals.

3. Highlight Your Academic Achievements

Don’t just list your degree. Include relevant coursework, projects, and academic honors.

4. Use Action Verbs

Use action verbs like "developed," "led," or "created" to convey your impact and initiative.

5. Tailor Your Resume for Each Job

Customize your resume to highlight skills and experiences relevant to the job.

6. Include Key Skills

List both technical and soft skills, such as programming, communication, and problem-solving.

7. Keep It Simple and Clear

Use a clean, professional layout with easy-to-read fonts.

8. Proofread for Perfection

Ensure your resume is error-free by thoroughly proofreading it for grammar and spelling mistakes.

9. Showcase Your CGPA (If Strong)

If your CGPA is above average, include it to highlight your academic performance.

10. Optimize for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)

Use industry-specific keywords from the job description to pass through ATS filters.

11. Include Your LeetCode or GitHub Profile

Showcase your problem-solving skills or coding projects by linking to your profile.

12. Highlight Relevant Projects

Showcase projects relevant to the job you're applying for.
